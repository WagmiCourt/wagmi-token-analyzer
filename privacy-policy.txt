Privacy Policy for Wagmi Token Analyzer

Effective Date: May 7, 2025

Thank you for using Wagmi Token Analyzer, a Chrome extension developed by the WAGMI Court community to promote transparency and security in Web3. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle information when you use our extension.

1. Information We Collect
Wagmi Token Analyzer does not collect or store personally identifiable information.
The extension operates entirely within your browser and processes data related to token activity in real time without transmitting personal data to any external servers.

We may collect non-personal, aggregated data such as:

Usage metrics (e.g., how often features are used)

Anonymized error logs (for debugging and improvement)

This data, if collected, is used solely to improve the performance and security of the extension.

2. Use of Data
Any data collected is used for the following purposes:

To improve and optimize the extension's features

To detect bugs, enhance security, and prevent misuse

To provide relevant insights about token behavior on-chain

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties.

3. Third-Party Services
Wagmi Token Analyzer may interact with third-party APIs or public blockchain data to provide token analysis. We do not control how these third parties handle data, so we encourage users to review their respective privacy policies.

4. Data Security
We implement appropriate security measures to protect any non-personal data we collect. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of transmitted or stored information.
