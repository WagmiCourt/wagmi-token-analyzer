
# Privacy Policy for Wagmi Token Analyzer

Effective Date: May 2, 2025

Wagmi Token Analyzer is a Chrome extension designed to assist users in analyzing cryptocurrency tokens on bullx.io. We are committed to maintaining your privacy and protecting any information you may provide.

## Information Collection and Use

This extension does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) or sensitive user data. It operates locally within your browser to analyze token information on the pages you visit.

## Permissions

The extension requests the following Chrome permissions:
- **activeTab**: To access the currently open tab for analysis.
- **scripting**: To inject necessary scripts to retrieve and analyze token data.
- **host_permissions**: Only for the domain `*.bullx.io`, which is required for the extension's functionality.

These permissions are solely used to facilitate the core functionality of the extension.

## Data Sharing

We do **not** share, sell, or transfer any data to third parties.

## Analytics and Tracking

This extension does **not** use any analytics or tracking tools.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with an updated effective date.
